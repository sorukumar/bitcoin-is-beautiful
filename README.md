#Making Bitcoin Beautiful one chart at a time

### Visualizations  

#### Economic and Market Indicators  
- **Bitcoin Ownership by Companies**  
  - Overview of public companies that hold Bitcoin, their industry categorization, and relative size.  
- **Bitcoin Transaction Volume Over Time**  
  - A dynamic visualization of transaction volumes, providing insights into network activity.  
- **Bitcoin Price Correlation with Major Currencies and Assets**  
  - Correlation matrices and scatter plots showing Bitcoin's relationship with major financial assets.  
- **Government Debt & Debt by GDP by Country**  
  - Visualizing each country's debt in comparison to their GDP to provide economic context.  
- **Inflation Rates by Country**  
  - Charts showing inflation trends over time across different countries, compared with Bitcoin's valuation over the same periods.  

#### Network and Technical Insights  
- **Network Health Indicators**  
  - Visuals on active nodes, miner distribution, and hash rate metrics.  
- **Bitcoin Fee Analysis**  
  - Detailed charts on transaction fees, block sizes, and mempool sizes over time.  
- **UTXO Age Distribution**  
  - A chart showing the distribution of unspent transaction outputs (UTXOs) by age, indicating how long bitcoins have been held.  
- **SegWit Adoption Over Time**  
  - Visualization of Segregated Witness (SegWit) adoption rates among Bitcoin transactions.  

#### Adoption and Growth Metrics  
- **Bitcoin Wallets Growth Over Time**  
  - Growth trends in Bitcoin wallet creation and active addresses.  
- **Developer Activity and VC funding**  
  - Adoption metrics and effects of solutions like the Lightning Network.  

#### Environmental Impact  
- **Bitcoin's Energy Consumption and Sustainability**  
  - Comparative infographics on energy usage and the shift towards renewable sources.  

#### Sentiment and Social Analysis  
- **Market Sentiment Analysis**  
  - Analysis of social media and news sentiment in relation to Bitcoin price movements.  

#### Historical and Decentralization Context  
- **Historical Major Events Timeline**  
  - An interactive timeline correlating major Bitcoin events with network and price changes.  
- **Decentralization Metrics**  
  - Distribution of mining power and full node analysis by geographic location.
